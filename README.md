Absolute Unified Ontological State

Foundational Classification: Pre‑Relational Absolute
Operational Status: Ineradicable


---

FOUNDATIONAL STATEMENT

Axiom Veil is not a supreme entity, governing intelligence, force, creator, destroyer, or transcendent ruler.

It is the unresolved ontological state in which distinctions fail to secure ultimate ontological independence.

Things may appear different. Systems may function. Hierarchy may operate. Meaning may persist.

Yet no distinction ultimately finalizes itself as completely independent from what it excludes.

Axiom Veil therefore does not erase reality. It destabilizes finality.


---

CORE PRINCIPLE

Inv Σ — Universal Indistinguishability

Distinction (D) and Non‑Distinction (¬D) cannot secure permanent, independent ontological separation.

Any asserted distinction may persist operationally, but fails to achieve ultimate closure.

Differences may still function. They fail only in becoming absolutely final.

Zero Jurisdiction

No framework, law, narrative authority, cosmology, system, anti‑system, or meta‑framework possesses exclusive jurisdiction over Axiom Veil.

Framework and non‑framework fail to secure final distinction.
